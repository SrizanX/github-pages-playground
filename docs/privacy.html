Privacy Policy for XTRA PR71 Router Controller

Effective Date: April 30, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how XTRA PR71 Router Controller ("we," "our," or "the app") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application. XTRA PR71 Router Controller is designed to provide control and management functionality for XTRA PR71 pocket routers. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our app.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Router Login Credentials: We store your router's login credentials locally on your device to enable easy access to your router. These credentials are never transmitted to our servers.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Router Information: The app collects information from your router such as network settings, connected devices, SMS messages, and contacts that are stored on the router. This information is processed locally within the app and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • App Usage Data: Basic usage statistics may be collected to help us improve the app's functionality. This data is anonymized and doesn't include personally identifiable information.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the app's functionality
  • Allow you to access and control your XTRA PR71 router
  • Diagnose technical issues and optimize app performance
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Storage

All router credentials and settings are stored locally on your device using secure SharedPreferences storage. We do not maintain any servers that store your personal information or router data.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties. The app communicates directly with your router on your local network, and data remains on your device.

Your Choices and Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your saved credentials from the app at any time through the settings menu
  • Opt out of anonymous usage statistics (if implemented in future versions)
  • Request information about what data is stored on your device by the app

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the effective date.

Permissions

The app requires only the following permission:

  • Internet access: Required to communicate with your router over your local network
